Canada 2030 Series

What's changing?

Autonomous technology is transferring to buses.

Bus stops will transform into WiFi, info hubs and charging stations with interactive displays.

The 5G wireless network will allow for real-time vehicle-to-infrastructure communication.

This can improve traffic and prevent accidents.

Public transit will be fully electrified.

Buses will be equipped with wireless charging batteries and other forms of renewable energy.

Potential Implications

Will reduce social inequality

Social programs can be more easily implemented and city monitoring will possibly decline law-enforcement related incidents.

Increased demand for public transit

This stems from the demand for autonomous cars. In effect, unused parking lots can be repurposed for urban attractions.

Customized "smart" cities

Cities will have varying degrees of amenities, such as air and water quality.
